About This Website

\

This educational website was created as part of an English Composition course (ENG 1123) at Northwest Mississippi Community College. The content is based on a literature review examining the impact of technological advancements on children's well-being.

\ \

The site incorporates interactive elements to engage visitors while maintaining academic integrity through proper citation of all sources. Visual representations of data are based on research findings but are simplified for general audience comprehension.
