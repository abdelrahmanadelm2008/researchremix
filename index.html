Exploring the Digital Landscape of Childhood

How screen time, video games, and AI are shaping the next generation

In today's digital age, children are growing up surrounded by technology. While these advancements offer educational opportunities, they also raise important questions about their impact on children's social skills, cognitive development, and emotional well-being. This website explores the latest research to help parents, educators, and policymakers make informed decisions about technology use in childhood.
